A savannah sparrow, about five songs per minute, a song every 12 seconds. Its the standard song,_ take take take take-it eeeeeeeaaasssssssyyyyyyy_. Just the one song over and over, with little variation. Calming, satisfying, nice.

Background

Sora, marsh wren, yellow-headed blackbird, ruddy duck, American coot, pied-billed grebe.
