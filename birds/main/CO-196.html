A second gray flycatcher at dawn. This male sings six to nine, rough chu-WIP songs before his first high, thin teeah, 39 songs in all during this one-minute selection.

Three neighboring gray flycatchers sing in the predawn darkness here in Temple Canyon: CO-195, CO-196, CO-197.

Background

Chipping sparrow, American robin, black-headed grosbeak, roar of river down below.
