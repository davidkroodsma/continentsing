A dusky flycatcher cycling through his three different songs: PIT-it, pa-EET, PIT-it, prrdurnt in the first five seconds. It's that low-pitched prrdurnt that especially gives him away, together with two other sounds: The pa-EET rises distinctively, the other is the explosive PIT-it.

Background

Red-breasted nuthatch, Lincoln's sparrow, brown-headed cowbird, red squirrel, Swainson's thrush, yellow warbler
